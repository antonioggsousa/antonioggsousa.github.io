Biography

I’m a bioinformatician and microbial ecologist at the EcoBioTec research group, at the CiiMAR-UP. I’ve a BSc in Biology since 2015 and a MSc in Cell and Molecular Biology since 2017 both awarded by the Faculty of Sciences, University of Porto. During the last year, I increased my knowledge on bioinformatics completing with success 24 ECTS from a post-graduation studies on Bioinformatics and Computational Biology, FCUP.
